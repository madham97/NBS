Expansion of park Lauta

Разширение на парк Лаута
+−
Leaflet | © OpenStreetMap contributors
Plovdiv, Bulgaria
City population: 337900
Duration: 2014 – ongoing
Implementation status: Ongoing
Scale: Meso-scale: Regional, metropolitan and urban level
Project area: 80000 m2
Type of area: Public Greenspace Area
Last updated: October 2021

An ongoing extensive expansion of park Lauta in Plovdiv has been carried out. The primary nature-based means used in the project is tree planting. However, animals were also introduced to the habitat. The project aims at creating a suitable place for recreation, purifying the air of Plovdiv, and enriching the park's fauna (Ref. 2; Ref. 3; Ref. 7).

Project objectives

- Improve the air quality of Plovdiv (Ref. 3). - Provide a place for rest and recreation (Ref. 2) - Enrich the park fauna (Ref. 7)

Implementation activities

- Prior to the project, citizens were consulted face-to-face, and through a survey with 60 respondents, to get an idea of the largest issues in the park that needed to be addressed. - Planting trees - Building grey infrastructure, sports facilities, and playgrounds - In 2015, 50 pheasants were introduced to the park, donated by hunters.(Ref. 1; Ref. 2; Ref. 7). "Currently, the greenery with an area of ​​about 420 acres has the status of a forest , but with the ongoing afforestation actions, Lauta Park is about to become one of the largest urban green spaces in the country" (Ref. 7, 2015). -"...іn rесеnt уеаrѕ thе grееnеrу аt Lаutа hаѕ bееn іnсrеаѕеd bу 80 dесаrеѕ, іn whісh mоrе thаn 2,500 nеw trееѕ hаvе bееn рlаntеd аnd аn іrrіgаtіоn ѕуѕtеm hаѕ bееn buіlt. Fоr dесаdеѕ, оnе оf thе оldеѕt раrkѕ іn Рlоvdіv hаѕ bееn nеglесtеd, but іn thе lаѕt еіght уеаrѕ mаnу thіngѕ hаvе bееn dоnе іn іt rеlаtеd tо іtѕ ѕосіаlіzаtіоn аnd turnіng іt іntо а fаvоrіtе рlасе fоr rесrеаtіоn аnd ѕроrtѕ оf Рlоvdіv rеѕіdеntѕ" (Ref. 10, 2020). There is also a "proposal for the expansion of Lauta Park by 32 decares to the east to the executors of the amendment to the General Development Plan of Plovdiv" (Ref. 10, 2020).

Details on the roles of the organisations involved in the project

The improvement of the park has been prioritized "bу thе mауоr оf "Тrаkіа" Коѕtаdіn Dіmіtrоv, fоr whоm thе іmрrоvеmеnt оf thе раrk hаѕ bееn а рrіоrіtу іn thе wоrk ѕіnсе hіѕ fіrѕt tеrm іn оffісе" (Ref. 10). Volunteers have been involved in tree planting (Ref. 1). In 2015, 50 pheasants were introduced to the park, donated by hunters (Ref. 7).
